Walleto · Icon System v1.0

Atelier Marks.

A custom duotone icon system replacing the placeholder emoji in Walleto's account and category pickers. Drawn on a 24px grid with a 1.5px stroke, tinted fills, and a single accent beat per mark.

Designed for the "Digital Atelier" aesthetic — three semantic families (Indigo, Moss, Amethyst), no decorative lines, no gradients inside marks.
